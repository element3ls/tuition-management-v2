How To Read This

The Content module owns the syllabus hierarchy: Year to Subject to Chapter to Question. The Exam module is a separate publishing workflow because it has a private source PDF, AI processing, staff review, approval, and student-facing questions and answers. Each exam belongs to one subject and can be linked to one or more relevant chapters.

Students do not automatically see everything. When a student logs in, the system checks their account, group memberships, and access rules. Exam access normally inherits from the year or subject, while a direct exam grant supports exceptional releases. Published exams appear primarily on the subject page and secondarily on linked chapter pages.

Access is central

Year and subject grants flow down to exams. Direct exam grants are available when needed.

Files stay private

Exam source PDFs stay staff-only. Students receive only reviewed and published Q&A.

Logs create visibility

Admin changes are audited, while student actions such as viewing exams are tracked.
